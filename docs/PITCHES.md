# BSC Voice Pitches

For ElevenLabs text-to-speech generation. Plain prose, no markdown, no special characters. Numbers spelled where natural for cleaner audio. Acronyms spelled letter by letter (F D A, H A C C P).

**ElevenLabs settings:** Voice = warm mid-register male (Adam or Antoni). Stability 50–60%. Similarity 75%. Style 0–10%. Speed default.

If you supply seafood, meat, produce, or any food category, we want to hear from you. Bahamian fishermen and family island suppliers, deliver direct to Spiny Tail Processing Plant or message us to coordinate pickup. Reach me directly on WhatsApp at one, two four two, three five nine, zero two eight five. We will move fast, and we will pay you on time. That is our promise.

Thank you for considering Bahamian Seafood Connection.

## 3. Full Systems Partner Pitch (~2 minutes 20 seconds)

For when partners want to understand the entire BSC operation end to end. Sends a strong signal of professionalism and operational maturity.

```
Hello, my name is Dedrick Storr, founder of Bahamian Seafood Connection.

I want to walk you through the complete system we have built, what every piece does, and how everything is accounted for, because trust starts with transparency.

Bahamian Seafood Connection operates as three coordinated arms.

First, our local marketplace. A full e commerce storefront, two point of sale registers in Nassau and Andros, and a wholesale arm. Every customer order, every register sale, every inventory movement is tracked in real time on our dashboard.

Second, the Spiny Tail Processing Plant on Firetrail Road in Nassau. Thirty thousand pounds of cold storage capacity. We have both a blast freezer and a holding freezer. This is where Bahamian seafood gets received, processed, graded by size, and packaged for export.

Third, B N T Inheritance, our partner entity that holds the F D A Food Facility Registration and the wholesale license required for legal export to the United States. We are cleared to ship into the U S today.

Behind all of this is a custom dashboard system that controls every operation. We have a boat intake form that logs every pound received with the captain name, the boat registration, the source island, the weight, and the cost paid. We have a yield measurement page that captures real measured output, never assumptions. We have a landed cost calculator for international sourcing decisions, including Bahamas customs duty by category. We print export labels in three languages, English, Creole, and Spanish, with Q R codes that let any buyer trace every case back to the original source boat and processing date.

For accountability, we track every dollar. Accounts payable, payroll, customer history, supplier balances, and per shipment profit and loss are all maintained in our reporting layer. We commit to net thirty day payment terms with every supplier, and we honor that commitment.

For partners like you specifically, we generate a private partner portal link, sent through WhatsApp, that lets you see your own data. Your outstanding balance, your payment history, soon your inventory in our freezer, and per shipment performance. No login required. No friction. Just transparency.

Last lobster season, in just thirty days, we sold over one hundred and eight thousand dollars worth of graded lobster tails to one buyer in Miami. That is the proven track record. We are ready to scale that volume significantly.

If you partner with us, you get a documented, professional, internationally compliant operation. You see the numbers. You see the inventory. You see the cold chain. You see the people doing the work.

Reach me on WhatsApp at one, two four two, three five nine, zero two eight five. I am ready to talk.

Thank you for your time.
```

## How to use

1. **Pick which pitch fits the audience.**
   - New supplier (fisherman or wholesaler) → Pitch 1
   - First touch with a sophisticated partner → Pitch 2
   - Sit-down meeting or formal proposal → Pitch 3

2. **Generate one MP3 per pitch in ElevenLabs.** Save with descriptive names: `bsc-supplier-pitch.mp3`, `bsc-partner-pitch-short.mp3`, `bsc-partner-pitch-full.mp3`.

3. **Send via WhatsApp as a voice note.** Voice notes get heard. Email attachments often don't.

4. **Update these pitches as the business evolves.** When BSC closes its first Igloo deal, update Pitch 3 to say "we already operate through Igloo Express." When the conch quota is approved, mention it. The pitches should stay current.
