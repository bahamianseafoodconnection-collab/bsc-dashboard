## 1. What BSC is

Bahamian Seafood Connection (BSC) is a family-owned Bahamian food business operating across three coordinated arms:

- **Local marketplace and POS** — public-facing storefront serving Nassau and the Family Islands with fresh seafood, meat, produce, and pantry staples. Web + mobile + retail.
- **Spiny Tail Processing Plant (Nassau)** — operational core. 30,000 lb cold storage capacity (10×10 ft blast freezer + 10×20 ft holding freezer). Processes whole seafood into export-ready and retail-ready product. Located on Firetrail Road, Nassau.
- **BNT Inheritance** — separate legal entity holding the FDA Food Facility Registration and US wholesale license required to export to the United States. Owned by three partners: Dedrick Storr (founder), Bill Casale (strategist), and Tom Gotthelf (investor).

Founded and operated by **Dedrick Tamico Storr Snr** and **Jaquel Rolle-Storr** with a Bahamian-resident staff of 9.

## 2. What BSC sells

**Seafood** (primary revenue):
- Spiny Lobster (*Panulirus argus*) — whole and tail, graded by size (5oz / 6oz / 7oz / 8oz / 9oz / 10–12oz / 12–14oz / 14–16oz)
- Conch — whole and cleaned (tenderized at Spiny Tail)
- Snapper varieties — mutton, lane, large, small
- Hog fish, grouper, mahi
- Salmon (imported, portion-controlled), shrimp (jumbo grades 16/20 and 25/30)

**Meat:** beef tenderloin, NY strip, ribeye (CAB), pork, poultry — primarily sourced from US wholesalers (duty-free into Bahamas).

**Grocery:** dry goods, beverages, dairy, produce, frozen.

**Branded retail-pack lines:** Sea Best (snapper finger, salmon portions, shrimp), Tropic Seafood, Father & Son.

- TPG Bahamas Distribution, Bahama Breeze, Caribbean Bottling, Promocean International, Asa H Pritchard, BWA, BJs Wholesale, Sysco, Sam's Club, and ~10 others for category-specific supply

**USA suppliers** (for duty-free or low-duty categories):
- Sam's Club, BJ's Wholesale, Costco, Walmart (Florida)
- Igloo Express (Miami) — bonded seafood trading hub (see section 4)

**Lobster sourcing strategy:** Bahamian fishermen typically tail their catch at sea (whole-lobster export is restricted). BSC buys lobster TAILS directly from Family Island day boats — Moores Island, Andros, others — at approximately $8/lb at season open ($1/lb cheaper than buying through a Nassau middleman). The 2026–2027 lobster season runs August through March.

**Conch sourcing strategy:** April–July is closed lobster season; conch supply is high and prices fall to ~$5/lb. August–March prices rise to ~$6.50/lb as fishermen prioritize lobster. BSC's freezer arbitrage: buy April–July, hold through season opening, sell August–October.

## 4. USA export pipeline (the wealth engine)

BSC ships processed lobster tails and other premium seafood to **Igloo Express in Miami, Florida** — a USA-bonded seafood trading hub that provides four leverages:

1. **Sell channel:** BSC stores product in Igloo's bonded facility and sells to global buyers (USA, EU, Asia, Caribbean) through Igloo's marketplace. Igloo IS the USA buyer CRM — BSC does not need to maintain individual buyer relationships.
2. **Source channel:** BSC buys from Igloo's global supplier network at competitive prices, replacing some local Bahamas sourcing on snow crab, shrimp, salmon, and snapper fillets.
3. **Bonded storage:** Products held in US bond pay no US customs duty until they leave bond into US commerce. Selling to Europe from Igloo means no US duty.
4. **Liquidity / price discovery:** Like a stock exchange for seafood. List product, buyers come.

**Igloo deal terms (locked in writing pending):**
- Processing fee: $1.75/lb (final pack, plastic sleeve, label, box)
- Commission: % of sale (exact pct pending)
- Storage: $700–$1,400/month flat (square-foot based)
- Possible advance financing: Igloo may pay BSC the lobster cost basis at the Spiny Tail door, allowing BSC to keep buying from fishermen without cash flow constraints

**Proven historical sales** (Spiny Tail → Jomara Seafood, Hialeah FL):
- Oct–Nov 2025: $108,354 across 7,025 lbs of lobster tail in ~30 days
- Per-grade pricing actuals: #1 quality 5–9oz at $16.25–17.25/lb, larger 10–16oz at $13–14/lb; #2 quality 5–9oz at $16.25/lb, 12–16oz at $12/lb
- Average sell price: $15.42/lb tail

**Current season projection (8 months Aug 2026 – March 2027):**
- 30,000 lbs tail/month at full Spiny Tail capacity = $849K gross at 10% Igloo commission
- 60,000 lbs tail/month with Igloo financing-enabled scaling = $1.7M gross
- Per-lb economics: $8 buy + $0.50 BSC variable + $1.75 Igloo pack + $0.09 storage + 10% commission = $3.54/lb net to BSC at $15.42 sell

## 6. Pricing principles

BSC operates **sacred pricing rules** by sales channel. These are operational law — they govern every product unless explicitly overridden:

| Channel | Margin | + VAT |
|---|---:|---|
| Nassau POS | 38% | + 10% VAT |
| Andros POS | 43% | + 10% VAT |
| Online Market | 25% | + 10% VAT |
| Local Wholesale | 12% | + 10% VAT |
| US Resale | 12% | + cost basis includes duty + freight |
| Bill Payments | 4.5% + $6 service fee | no VAT |
| Bill Casale | 5% | no VAT (sacred — never lower) |

**Per-SKU overrides** are allowed for premium / portion-controlled / branded products that command above-formula market prices (snapper finger Sea Best, jumbo shrimp, salmon portions, etc.). Sacred rules remain the floor; overrides only push price UP based on real market prices already validated by customers.

**Yield discipline:** all product cost-basis math is computed from REAL measured yields, never assumptions. Whole-fish-to-fillet, whole-conch-to-meat, whole-lobster-to-tail conversions are recorded per batch in the system.

## 7. Payment terms

**BSC commits to Net-30 payment terms across all suppliers.** This is in writing and surfaced on every partner's portal view. Goal: predictable, on-time payment is BSC's competitive advantage in a fragmented Bahamian supplier market where many distributors run unpredictable.

**BSC accepts from customers:** RBC Plug & Pay (Visa / Mastercard), cash on delivery, cash at POS, mailboat COD for Family Islands. Future: Apple Pay / Google Pay / international wire for wholesale buyers.

## 8. Trust and regulatory compliance

- **FDA Food Facility Registration:** held via BNT Inheritance partnership entity. Required for USA export. Active.
- **HACCP plan:** in place at Spiny Tail.
- **Bahamian Wholesale License:** held via BNT Inheritance.
- **Conch export quota application:** filed with Director Lester Gittens (Bahamas Department of Marine Resources). 50,000 lbs requested.
- **Traceability:** every export case carries a QR code linking to the per-lot history (boat name, captain, catch date, processing date, freezer location, expiry date = processing + 24 months).
- **Trilingual labeling:** all export-grade case labels include English + Bahamian/Haitian Creole + Spanish content for the Caribbean and Hispanic-American markets.
- **Cold-chain integrity:** managed at Spiny Tail's blast and holding freezers, plus a 3,000 lb capacity at the Cetas variety store, Andros.

## 10. How to engage as a USA wholesale partner

1. Visit Igloo Express's BSC inventory listings (when listed) or contact directly via WhatsApp.
2. Specify product, grade (#1 or #2), size grade (5oz–16oz), and quantity.
3. Receive a quote in USD per lb FOB Igloo Miami.
4. Confirm purchase; Igloo handles fulfillment from BSC's bonded inventory.
5. Receive trilingual case labels with QR-code traceability.
